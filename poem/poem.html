My Poem

Ooh

I saw my life branching like a fig tree,
each future hanging quietly in front of me.
A home, a love, a family warm, 
a poet’s voice, a different form.

A teacher speaking bright ideas,
a traveler drifting through the years.
So many lives I wished to try,
so many dreams I couldn’t deny.

But I sat there, unable to choose,
afraid that I would always lose.
And one by one, the figs fell down,
soft and silent, without a sound.
